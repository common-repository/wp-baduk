== Description ==

This plugin allows you to use Sensei's library style text input to draw
baduk diagrams.  

== Installation ==

1. Upload folder wp-baduk to /wp-content/plugins/
2. Turn on plugin
3. Add diagrams by [baduk] [/baduk]

== Example == 

[baduk]
$$W Double ko
$$  ------------------
$$ | . 6 . . . . . . .
$$ | 3 O X X X . . . .
$$ | . O O X O X . . .
$$ | 1 X X O . . . . .
$$ | 2 . X O . O . . .
$$ | . X X O . O . . .
$$ | 4 O O X X . . . .
$$ | 5 O X . . . . . .
$$ | . . O . . . . . .
$$ | . . . , . . . . .
[/baduk]
